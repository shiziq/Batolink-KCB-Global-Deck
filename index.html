The Diagnostic

Why KCB Agents
Are Not Growing

We studied the KCB Mtaani network across Nairobi, Nakuru, and Mombasa. Four problems emerged everywhere.

  • 01
    Long Queues
    Customers see a queue, they walk. Transaction lost forever. Human tellers create unavoidable bottlenecks.
  • 02
    Cash Dependency
    Cash has no audit trail. Security risks at every touchpoint. Revenue leakage that cannot be traced.
  • 03
    High Wallet Charges
    Fees from wallet to branch kill agent margins. Agents stop promoting KCB. Customers find cheaper alternatives.
  • 04
    Manual Processes
    Labor costs eat thin margins. Zero differentiation against M-Pesa agents. No unique value proposition.
