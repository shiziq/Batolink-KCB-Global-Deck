## TECHNICAL NOTES

### Particle Canvas
- 80 floating green/gold particles behind all slides
- Particles connect with lines when close
- Mouse movement repels nearby particles
- Creates a living, breathing background

### 3D Tilt Cards (Slide 4)
- Cards tilt in 3D perspective on hover
- Transform: translateY + rotateX
- Glass-morphism effect with backdrop blur

### Counter Animations (Slides 1, 7)
- Numbers count up with easing when slide becomes active
- Uses cubic ease-out for smooth deceleration
- Formats as "KSh X.XB+" or "KSh XM"

### Video (Slide 8)
- Replace `assets/Bato video.mp4` with your actual demo video
- Auto-resets when leaving the slide
- Click play button to start

### Logo Watermark
- Fixed top-left corner showing both KCB and Batolink logos
- Fades slightly, brightens on hover
- Present on every slide

### Scanlines
- Subtle CRT scanline overlay for cinematic texture
- Very low opacity (3%) — adds depth without distraction

### Navigation
- Arrow keys, space bar, swipe gestures
- Click dots to jump
- Mouse wheel not enabled (prevents accidental skips)
