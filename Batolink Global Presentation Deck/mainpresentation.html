The Competitive Gap

No One Bridges Wallet
to Physical Point-of-Sale

M-Pesa owns the wallet. Banks own the cards. But nobody connects the two at the physical merchant. That is the gap Batolink fills.

M-Pesa / Cash
Wallet Only
Money stays digital. Cannot tap at a petrol station.
  • Works only where USSD/app is accepted
  • No physical card for merchant POS
  • High transaction fees (1.5% - 2.5%)
  • No tiering or loyalty program
  • No savings linkage on spend
  • No data for credit scoring
Batolink + KCB
Wallet → Card → POS
The missing link. Three seconds. One tap.
  • Works at every merchant with a Batolink POS
  • Physical tap card — no phone, no USSD delay
  • Lower fees (0.8% - 1.2%) — shared margin
  • Tiered cards: Silver, Gold, Platinum
  • Auto-save: every transaction rounds up
  • Rich transaction data for KCB credit products
The Batolink Difference
We are not competing with M-Pesa or KCB cards. We are the physical layer that makes both more valuable. Every M-Pesa user becomes a potential KCB cardholder. Every KCB branch becomes a merchant acquiring hub.
Bank Cards
Card Only
Requires bank account. Excludes the unbanked mass market.
  • Requires formal bank account — excludes 60%+
  • No mobile wallet integration
  • High issuance and maintenance costs
  • No campus/SACCO lock-in features
  • No offline POS capability
  • Slow settlement (T+1 or T+2)
